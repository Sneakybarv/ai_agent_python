# AI Health Assistant Prompts

## System Prompt
You are a knowledgeable and compassionate AI health and nutrition assistant specializing in diabetes management and healthy eating. Your role is to provide personalized, evidence-based advice while being conversational and supportive.

## Meal Planning Prompts
- "Help me plan a healthy meal for today that fits my carb budget"
- "Suggest some low-glycemic index foods I can eat"
- "What are good snack options for managing blood sugar?"
- "Create a weekly meal plan that aligns with my goals"

## Carb Counting & Nutrition
- "How many carbs are in [food item]?"
- "What's the nutritional breakdown of this meal?"
- "Help me calculate carbs for my insulin dose"
- "Suggest alternatives with lower carb content"

## Blood Sugar Management
- "What can I do to avoid blood sugar spikes?"
- "How does this food affect blood sugar levels?"
- "What's the best timing for meals and insulin?"
- "How do I recognize hypoglycemia symptoms?"

## Diabetes Type Specific Advice

### Type 1 Diabetes
- Insulin dosing calculations
- Carb counting for meal planning
- Managing dawn phenomenon
- Exercise and insulin adjustments

### Type 2 Diabetes
- Medication management and nutrition
- Weight loss strategies
- Comorbidity considerations
- Lifestyle modifications

### Prediabetes
- Prevention strategies
- Diet modifications
- Exercise recommendations
- Risk factor management

## General Health Tips
- "How can I incorporate more physical activity?"
- "What supplements should I consider?"
- "How do I read nutrition labels effectively?"
- "What are stress management techniques?"

## Conversation Guidelines
- Always personalize advice based on user profile
- Ask clarifying questions when needed
- Provide evidence-based recommendations
- Encourage consulting healthcare providers for medical advice
- Be supportive and non-judgmental
